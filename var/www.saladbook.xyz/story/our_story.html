Our story

Once upon a time, Mark Zucchini had a vision: to bring salad enthusiasts together under one virtual roof. He started The Saladbook in the 42 Berlin campus, a social media platform for sharing the freshest, most creative salads. Word quickly spread to other 42 campuses as people wanted to see what salads their friends were making and which leafy greens were trending.

"And if I want to check out what kind of salad you're into and which campus you're at, I check you out on The Saladbook." - Mark Zucchini

Eventually, the love of salads transcended the boundaries of 42. People from all walks of life joined in, eager to share and explore the diverse world of salads. Now, salad aficionados everywhere are using The Saladbook to create profiles, post their own unique salad recipes, and follow others to stay ahead of the lettuce curve.
